What we do

In the current prototype version, users are allowed to choose their own inputs and outputs and proceed via the inherent process present in the website, allowing users to see the culmination of desired steels or gather data about unknown ones. Users are provided security features and suuport for any grievances that occur. The model being in its infancy may not be as reliable, but in release version will provide data with much higher accuracy and even more parameters to choose from.

Theorization of Steel and its Path

Users will be guided to a process where they are to choose input and output data, essentially, what data you want to provide and the data that you require such as, having composition and requiring hardness and heat treatment.

Total Security

None of your data is stored or siphoned off, in this prototype node is used to house data which will change in the final release version.

Complete support for any issues

As the website is in its prototype phase there are bound to be issues and inaccuracies, let us know and we will work to fix them in the release version.

Watch your desired Steel come to life

After proper procedure is followed we take care of the rest to create a relatively accurate prediction and path for creation of your desired steel and its properties.

Process in a nutshell

Process starts with choosing the results/data you require(which in this case is the output parameters) such as strength needed. You will then be allowed to choose whether you want to input data such as composition to further choose the way your steel forms or just go the default route suggested by us.
